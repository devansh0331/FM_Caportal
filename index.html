Welcome to
Chartered Accountant

Chartered Accountant is an eminent Chartered Accountant firm, registered under ICAI, managed by a team of professionals having a vintage of more than 2 decades of professional experience. The firm aims to provide end to end solutions to clients across all industries in financial sector. It caters a full bouquet of financial services which includes Audit & Assurance services, Accounting services, Taxation services, Legal services (Company related matters and other laws), Valuation services, Documentation services, Consultancy services and complete Business solutions ranging from the formation to its operations. The firm has a diverse portfolio of clients belonging to a varied range of sectors including Real Estate, Textile, FMCG, Leather, Manufacturing, Trading, Financial Sector, and also to Health Professionals, High Net worth Individuals (HNIs), NGOs.
